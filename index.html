Instructions

Each round, a video of somewhere on or around campus will play. It is your job to figure out where the video is before the time runs out. Use the map below the video to drop a pin at the correct location. Your score will be calculated based on how close you are to the correct location and how quickly you chose your answer. Make sure you click the Confirm Guess button to lock in your answer!
